§ 3   Projects

Some of my Projects

In Progress
KCL Mathematics Module Hub
When I was picking my modules for second year at King's College London, I felt that the student resource site KEATS didn't give students what they needed in one place — what a module unlocks, what prerequisites exist, how it's assessed, where it leads. What you pick in a Mathematics degree can make or break your experience. This tool has helped my cohort and others under the Department of Mathematics get the most from their university education.

This tool includes all of the following;
• Module Pre-Requisite Tool: Allows students to see what modules they can select in the future based off their current module selections.
• Module 'Gear Builder' Tool: Allows students to create a 'loadout' of modules they can take in the future, a more interactive alternative to the pre-requisite tool.
• Comprehensive Module Index: Allows students to browse modules and access their info pages.
• Individual Module Info Pages: Allows students to see a module's syllabus, lecture notes, pre-requisites, recommended readings and exam information, all in one place.
