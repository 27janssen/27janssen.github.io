§ 3 Projects

Some of my Projects

In Progress
KCL Maths Module Hub
When I was picking my modules for my second year of university at King's College London, I felt as if the student resource site, KEATS, didn't have the capability to tell students what they needed to know all in the right place — what a module unlocks, what you can do with it, what you need to do in order to do it, how it's assessed, what it can lead to. What modules you pick in a Mathematics degree can make or break your experience in the field, and a tool like this has helped my cohort and those on other courses under the Department of Mathematics optimise what they pick to make sure they get the most out of their university education.
